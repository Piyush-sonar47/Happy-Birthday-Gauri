The Day We Met

**Jya Divshi Aapan Bhetlo ❤️** To IMR College madhla ek normal divas hota, pan mala tevha mahiti ny hot ki ha divas mazhya ayushyat itka special honar aahe. College campus var mi tula pahilyanda pahila. Aajubajula khup lok hote, pan mazh laksh fakt tujhya kadech gel. Tujha to god smile baghun kahi tari veglach feeling zhala. Tyaveli suru zhaleli ek choti olakh, halu halu sundar aani khup khas naatyat badalli. Aaj mage valun baghitla ki vatata, IMR College madhli ti pahili bhet mazhya ayushyatil saglyat sundar goshtinpaiki ek hoti. Kadhi kadhi saglyat sundar premkatha ekhadyā saamanya thikanapasun suru hotat, ani aapli premkatha suru zali ti IMR College chya campus var. ❤️✨

Our First Date

**Our First Date ❤️** Aapli pahili date **Dear Momos ** madhye zali, ani to divas aajhi mazhya aathvanit titkach special aahe. ☕✨ Suruvat fakt ek casual bhet mhanun zali hoti, pan vel kasa gela kalalach nahi. Gappa, hasne ani ekmekansobat ghalavlela pratyek kshan khup sundar vatat hota. Tya divshi mala as vatla ki tu fakt ek special vyakti nahi, tar mazhya ayushyat khup mahatvachi honar aahes. ❤️ Aapan ekmekansobat itke ramlo hoto ki vel sampu naye asach vatat hota. To divas, tya gappa ani tuzha hasra chehra mazhya manat nehmich rahil. Our First Trip

**Our First Trip ❤️** Aapli pahili trip mazhya sathi khup special hoti, karan ti fakt ek pravas navhati — ti aaplya naatyachi suruvat adhik sundar banavnari aathvan hoti. ✨ Aapan doghe **Shegaon** la gelo hoto, **Shri Gajanan Maharaj** yanchya darshanala ani aaplya naatyasathi aashirwad ghenyasathi. 🙏❤️ To pravas, tya gappa, ekmekansobat ghalavlela vel ani mandirat ekatra kelele prarthana... saglach khup khas hota. Tya divshi mala janavla ki aapla naata fakta premach nahi, tar vishwas, aadar ani devacha aashirwad yanni suddha bharlela aahe. Gajanan Maharajanchya charani ubhe rahun aapan aaplya sundar bhavishyachi prarthana keli, ani to kshan mazhya hrudayat nehmich japun theven. ❤️ Aajhi Shegaonchi ti trip aathavli ki chehryavar hasu yet ani man punha tya sundar kshanamadhye harvun jat. 💖 *“Aapli pahili trip Shegaonchi hoti, pan khari gosht mhanje to pravas don hrudayanna adhik javal gheun ala.”* ❤️🙏✨
